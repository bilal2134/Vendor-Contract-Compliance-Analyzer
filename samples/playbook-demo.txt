4.2.1 Cyber Liability Coverage
Vendors must maintain at least $5000000 cyber liability coverage and reference the coverage obligation in the DPA for data-processing engagements.

2.7 Termination Standards
Termination periods must be consistent across governing commercial documents and ancillary vendor materials.

5.4.3 Data Retention Requirements
All data-processing vendors must commit to documented retention periods and deletion timelines aligned to company schedules.

3.9 Security Certifications
Security certifications such as SOC 2 or ISO 27001 must be identified in the security questionnaire and referenced in the MSA.
