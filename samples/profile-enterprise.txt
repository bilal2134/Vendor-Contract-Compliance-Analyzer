SECTION 4 – TERMINATION AND TRANSITION PROVISIONS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

4.1  Termination Notice Period
     NovaTech's standard commercial terms require 60 days written notice
     for termination for convenience by either party. This is consistent
     with Article 2.3 of MSA-2026-0047 signed with Greybeard.

     CLIENT ADVISORY: Greybeard's Procurement Policy Playbook (Section
     14.1) specifies a standard 90-day termination-for-convenience notice
     period. The Greybeard Legal team has reviewed and approved a 60-day
     notice period for this engagement under Exception Log EX-2024-113.
     Both parties acknowledge this deviation from Playbook standard.

     For termination for cause (material breach): 30-day cure period
     applies, consistent with MSA Article 2.4.

4.2  Exit Assistance and Data Portability
     Upon receipt of a termination notice, NovaTech will provide:
     (a)  90 days of complimentary transition assistance, including
          data export in client-specified formats (CSV, JSON, Parquet,
          Excel), API access for bulk data extraction, and workflow
          documentation transfer.
     (b)  Integration specification documentation for all active connectors.
     (c)  3 knowledge transfer sessions (2 hours each) with the
          implementation and data engineering teams.
     (d)  Cooperation with successor vendor onboarding, including joint
          sessions as reasonably requested.

4.3  Data Deletion Certificate
     Following complete data export and expiry of the 30-day post-
     termination retention window, NovaTech's DPO will issue a signed
     Certificate of Data Destruction per DPA Article 9.4 within
     5 business days of deletion completion.
